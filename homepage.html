Meet Chef Zachary Hutton

Chef Hutton comes from humble beginnings. Growing up on a farm in Apache, Oklahoma within an Indian reservation. From that upbringing, he has learned the importance of cultivation, community, creativity, use of homegrown products, farm-raised local food, and the value of hard work. His ideals of sustainability have carried him from a small town in Oklahoma to national recognition. He has been a semifinalist for the James Beard Foundation's Best Chef Southwest in 2022, appeared on Guy's Grocery Games, and became a Chopped Champion in 2024. Chef Zachary has maintained a passion for his craft and the history behind how a recipe has been developed. Zach has taken his classical French training and Oklahoma heritage to create a food experience like never before. That combination has led to a unique and truly haute cuisine experience.
